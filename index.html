Economic Equity Initiative

Student Organization

EEI is a student-led organization that flips the script on traditional finance. While most clubs focus on personal wealth, we ask how financial knowledge can empower communities that have been left behind.

Whether you're interested in the stock market, community development, or international economics, EEI provides the skills to make a real-world impact.

What We Do

Market Analysis.

Studying how large-scale capital can be directed toward ethical and equitable companies through socially responsible investing — because the stock market can be a force for good.

Nonprofit Consulting.

Partnering with local nonprofits to provide real financial support — from budget analysis to grant research. Because we help the helpers.
